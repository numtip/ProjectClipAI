# Video Motion Prompt Template

```
Scene:
Subject:
Style:
Camera:
Lighting:
Motion:
Negative:
Duration:
Aspect Ratio:
```

### Example

```
Scene: A neon-lit cyberpunk street at night, rain falling, reflections on wet pavement
Subject: A figure in metallic armor walking slowly toward camera
Style: Cyberpunk, cinematic, Blade Runner aesthetic, high contrast
Camera: Steady dolly forward, slight low angle
Lighting: Neon blue and pink ambient light, rain droplets catching light
Motion: Figure walking forward, coat swaying slightly, rain falling steadily, neon signs flickering
Negative: Blurry, flickering, distorted face, jittery motion, color banding
Duration: 5
Aspect Ratio: 16:9
```
